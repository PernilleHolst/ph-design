About me

For many years I've been drawing and creating digital illustrations using Clip Studio Paint and Affinity to bring me and my clients ideas to life. Throughout the years have I been introduced to more graphic designs, which I've come to learn about Figma. From here I had small gigs and designs I made, and that inspired me to continue this path, from which I then applied for multimedia designer class. This journey is to not only get to learn a lot more about this path, but also to inspire my desire of working as a UI/UX designer and hone my skills through this and learn about designing websites / apps that are not only beautiful and well designed, but also to create amazing user experiences.
